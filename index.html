// Who We Are

About MeraIPU

Built by GGSIPU students, for GGSIPU students. Here's what drives us.

🎯

OUR MISSION

To eliminate scattered resources and exam panic. MeraIPU centralises every subject list, study link, and exam resource so students spend time learning — not searching.

⚡

WHAT WE OFFER

Semester-wise subject listings across all GGSIPU engineering branches. Smart prep tracking with curated YouTube playlists, NPTEL courses, notes, and PYQs.

🔬

THE TECHNOLOGY

Pure HTML, CSS & JavaScript. No frameworks, no bloat. Built lean for fast load times on any device, including low-bandwidth college networks.

🌱

GROWING PLATFORM

We're continuously adding PYQs, topic-wise playlists, faculty notes, and syllabus roadmaps. More branches. More semesters. More resources.
